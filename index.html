Executive Profile

With over two decades of international experience in the hospitality and maritime sectors, I have dedicated my career to the pursuit of operational excellence and transformative leadership.

My journey began in the vibrant hospitality landscapes of India, moving through the competitive markets of the Middle East, and eventually spanning the globe aboard some of the world's most prestigious cruise lines, including Holland America Line and Royal Caribbean. As a resident of Mumbai, I bring a global perspective back to my home base, blending international standards with local expertise.

Throughout my tenure as a General Manager and Food & Beverage Director, I have specialized in turning around underperforming operations and building high-performance teams from the ground up. My philosophy centers on the "Team Builder" approach—recognizing that the foundation of any 5-star guest experience is a motivated, well-trained, and empowered staff.

In the modern era, I have pivoted towards integrating AI-driven automation into traditional hospitality frameworks. By leveraging tools like n8n and autonomous agents, I aim to streamline back-of-house operations, allowing leadership to focus on what truly matters: the guest. This portfolio serves as a testament to that bridge between seasoned executive experience and future-forward technology.
