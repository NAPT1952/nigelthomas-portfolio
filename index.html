Full Biography

Nigel Anthony Thomas is a seasoned hospitality executive with over 20 years of international experience across the full spectrum of the industry—from luxury resorts and fine-dining restaurants to high-volume cruise lines. His career began on the high seas, where he managed operations under strict USPH and ServSafe standards, honing his ability to deliver excellence in fast-paced, multicultural environments.

Returning to land, Nigel took on General Manager roles at prestigious properties in India, including a resort at Nandi Hills and a patisserie in Hyderabad. He has consistently delivered double-digit improvements in profitability and guest satisfaction while reducing staff turnover through mentorship and internal promotions. His expertise spans P&L management, pre-opening projects, operational turnarounds, and building stable, high-performing teams.

Recognized for his strategic vision and hands-on leadership, Nigel is passionate about creating unforgettable guest experiences and sustainable business growth. He holds certifications in FSSAI, HACCP, and ServSafe, and continues to develop his skills through international hospitality networks.
